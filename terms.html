1. About InfraMint

InfraMint is an online art marketplace connecting emerging artists with collectors worldwide. By using our platform, you agree to these terms.

2. Accounts

You must provide accurate information when creating an account. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials. InfraMint reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms.

3. Artists

Artists must complete a verification process before listing artwork for sale. All artwork listed must be original work created by the artist. Mass-produced prints, reproductions, and AI-generated images presented as physical originals are prohibited.

  • Artists are responsible for accurately describing their artwork including medium, dimensions, and condition.
  • Artists must ship artwork within the timeframe agreed upon after a sale.
  • Artists must provide a valid postal shipping quote when an order is placed.

4. Collectors

Collectors agree to pay the listed artwork price plus shipping and applicable customs/import duties. Payment is held in escrow until delivery is confirmed.

5. Commission Structure

InfraMint operates on the following commission structure:

  • Gallery (InfraMint): 30% of the artwork sale price
  • Partner (if applicable): 5% of the artwork sale price for the first 3 sales, then at the artist's discretion
  • Artist: Receives the remainder (65% with partner, 70% without)

Shipping costs and customs duties are paid by the collector and passed through to the artist. They are not subject to commission.

6. Payments & Escrow

All payments are processed securely. Funds are held in escrow until the collector confirms receipt of the artwork. If a dispute arises, InfraMint will mediate according to our dispute resolution process.

7. Shipping

Artists are responsible for packaging and shipping artwork safely. Shipping costs are determined by the artist based on a real postal quote after the order is placed. Tracking information must be provided.

8. Digital Art & Editions

Digital artworks may be sold as limited editions. The artist specifies the number of copies available. Once all editions are sold, the artwork is marked as sold out. Each edition buyer receives the same digital file.

9. Intellectual Property

Artists retain copyright to their work. By listing on InfraMint, artists grant the platform a non-exclusive license to display and promote their artwork. Collectors receive ownership of the physical or digital artwork but not reproduction rights unless explicitly stated.

10. Prohibited Content

The following are prohibited on InfraMint:

  • Mass-produced or factory-made artwork presented as original
  • Stolen or plagiarized work
  • Offensive, illegal, or harmful content
  • Misleading descriptions or images

11. Partner Program

Partners who recruit artists to InfraMint earn a 5% commission on their recruited artists' first 3 sales. After 3 sales, the artist may choose to continue or discontinue the partner commission. Partners must accept the Partner Program Agreement separately.
