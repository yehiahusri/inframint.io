Our Mission

Too much great art sits unseen in studios, dorm rooms, and storage spaces. We built InfraMint to change that — a simple way for emerging artists to reach collectors directly, without gallery gatekeepers or complicated logistics.

The Story

InfraMint started with a simple observation: talented art students graduate with incredible work — and no way to sell it.

Traditional galleries take 50% commissions and won't look at emerging artists. Online marketplaces are crowded with print-on-demand products. There was no clean, artist-friendly way to sell original work.

So we built one. A platform that handles the hard parts — payments, shipping logistics, buyer trust — so artists can focus on what they do best: creating.
