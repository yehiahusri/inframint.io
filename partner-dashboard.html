Partner program agreement

InfraMint Partner Program Agreement

1. Commission Structure: Partners earn 5% commission on sales made by artists they have recruited to InfraMint. The gallery retains 30% of each sale, the partner receives 5%, and the artist receives the remaining 65%.

2. Automatic Commission: Commission is automatically applied to the first 3 sales of each recruited artist. After the 3rd sale, the artist has the option to continue or discontinue the partner commission.

3. Artist's Choice: If an artist chooses to remove the partner commission after the 3rd sale, the partner will be notified. The commission split will then revert to 30% gallery / 70% artist for future sales.

4. Recruitment: Partners may recommend artists to join InfraMint. All artists must pass the platform's verification process independently. Partner recommendations do not guarantee acceptance.

5. Payment: Commissions are paid out to the partner's designated bank account or PayPal after each completed sale and confirmed delivery.

6. Transparency: Commission details are visible to the partner, the artist, and InfraMint administration. No hidden fees or charges apply.

7. Termination: InfraMint reserves the right to terminate a partner's account for violations of platform policies, fraudulent activity, or misrepresentation.

8. Modifications: InfraMint may update these terms with 30 days' notice to active partners.
