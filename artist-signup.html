Artist Agreement

By joining InfraMint as an artist, you agree to the following:

  • I guarantee that all artwork I list is original and created by me.
  • I am the sole owner of the artwork and have the right to sell it.
  • I will not sell additional copies beyond any declared edition limits.
  • I understand that fraud or misrepresentation may result in account suspension, withheld payments, and potential legal action.

Commission & Payments

  • InfraMint charges a 30% commission on each sale.
  • Payments are held in escrow until the buyer confirms receipt.
  • Payouts are processed within 5 business days of buyer confirmation.
  • VAT will be applied where required by law.

Shipping Responsibilities

  • I am responsible for packaging artwork professionally and securely.
  • I will obtain a shipping quote from my local courier after a sale.
  • I will ship within 5 business days of payment confirmation.
  • I will provide tracking information for all shipments.
