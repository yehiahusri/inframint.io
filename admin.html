🔍 Verification Checklist
Before approving, check for AI-generated images:
✓ Does the handwritten note look real? (AI can't fake specific handwriting)
✓ Does the photo have natural lighting and shadows?
✓ Check hands/fingers in the photo (AI often gets these wrong)
✓ Is there readable text in the background? (AI mangles text)
✓ Right-click image → save → upload to illuminarty.ai for free AI detection
✓ Check image metadata: exifdata.com — real photos have camera data, AI images don't

Delivery Reminders

Track shipped orders and send automated reminders to collectors

How It Works

When an order is shipped, the collector has 7 days to confirm delivery. This page shows all shipped orders and lets you send reminder emails. After 7 days, you can release payment to the artist.

Daily routine: Open this page once a day, click "Send All Pending Reminders" to email every collector who hasn't confirmed yet.
