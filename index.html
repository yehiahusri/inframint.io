How It Works
3 steps
01

Upload your work

Photograph your artwork, set your price, and list it in minutes. We'll estimate shipping costs automatically.

02

Someone buys it

When a collector purchases, you get the real shipping quote from your local post office. They pay art + shipping.

03

Ship & get paid

Upload the postal invoice, ship with tracking. Once delivered, the funds release to you. Simple.
